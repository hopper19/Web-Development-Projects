About New York City

New York City is the largest city in the United States, with a population of over 8 million people. It is known for its iconic landmarks, such as the Statue of Liberty, Central Park, and the Empire State Building. It is also a hub for business, finance, and entertainment, with Broadway shows and world-renowned museums like the Metropolitan Museum of Art.
